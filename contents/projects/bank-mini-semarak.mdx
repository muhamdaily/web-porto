**Bank Mini Semarak** is a web-based financial management application developed to support school financial operations at **SMK PGRI 5 Jember**. The system centralizes various financial processes such as SPP payments, department data management, additional fees, PTK payments, and comprehensive financial reporting.

---

## The Story Behind Bank Mini Semarak

School financial management often involves multiple data types and repetitive administrative tasks that can be difficult to manage manually. SMK PGRI 5 Jember required a reliable digital system to streamline payment processes, maintain accurate records, and generate structured financial reports.

Bank Mini Semarak was built to address these needs by providing a centralized platform where administrators can manage student payments, departmental data, and staff-related transactions efficiently. With a clean interface and structured workflows, the system helps reduce errors, improve transparency, and simplify daily financial administration.

---

## Key Features

* Dashboard overview of school financial activities
* Student data and user account management
* Department (Jurusan) data management with configurable SPP rates
* SPP payment recording and monitoring
* Additional fee payment management
* PTK payment and data management
* Detailed financial reports (SPP, additional fees, savings, arrears, PTK)
* Role-based access control (admin, operator)
* Responsive and modern UI using TailwindCSS
