**PMB Universitas Islam Jember** is a web-based student admission system designed to digitize and simplify the new student enrollment process. The platform enables prospective students to register online, submit required data, and access admission information efficiently through a structured and user-friendly interface.

---

## The Story Behind PMB Universitas Islam Jember
