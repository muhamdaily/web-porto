**KuyBisnis** is a web-based application developed to support the **Agribusiness Entrepreneurship course at the Faculty of Agriculture (FAPERTA), Universitas Muhammadiyah Jember**. The platform helps students plan, develop, and evaluate agribusiness ideas in a structured and practical way.

---

### The Story Behind KuyBisnis

In agribusiness entrepreneurship education, students are required not only to understand theory but also to apply it through real business planning and execution. However, managing business plans, documentation, and progress evaluation manually can be inefficient.

KuyBisnis was created as a digital solution to support learning activities in the **Agribusiness Entrepreneurship course**, providing students with a centralized platform to organize business ideas, prepare business plans, and track their entrepreneurial progress throughout the semester.

---

### Key Features

* Agribusiness entrepreneurship dashboard
* Motivational and learning video integration
* Business category and product management
* Agribusiness business plan creation
* Value proposition documentation
* Business legality document upload
* Business progress tracking and evaluation
* Lecturer monitoring and assessment support
* Certificate generation for completed business projects
* Responsive and modern user interface
