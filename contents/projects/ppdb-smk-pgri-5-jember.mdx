**PPDB SMK PGRI 5 Jember** is a web-based new student admission system designed to digitize and streamline the enrollment process. The platform allows prospective students to register online, submit personal and academic data, and access registration information efficiently.

---

## The Story Behind PPDB SMK PGRI 5 Jember

The new student admission process in many schools is still handled manually, making data management inefficient and prone to errors. SMK PGRI 5 Jember required a reliable digital system to manage applicant data while improving accessibility for students and parents.
